Featured Work

MATE Website Redesign

Mario Testino’s art institute MATE in Lima is a not-for-profit centre which aims to support and celebrate Peruvian artists while offering international and contemporary art through exhibitions and events. Together with MARIOTESTINO+ and Future Corp, No Plans designed and developed a website for MATE that is fresh and engaging and complements Mario Testino’s playful photographic style. mate.pe

+

Zanzan E-Commerce

Zanzan is a London-based luxury eyewear brand which produces high-quality, handmade women’s sunglasses in a range of fashion-led designs for a worldwide retail audience. No Plans successfully transformed the brand's website into a fully functioning online shop suitable for mobile devices. The finished site retains Zanzan’s free-spirited character while creating an enjoyable and user-friendly retail experience. zanzan.co.uk

Are.na

The collaborative research platform hosts a growing community that interacts by adding, connecting and organising pieces of visual information including images, links, files and text. As a co-founder, Daniel Pianetti has worked with Are.na’s fellow team members on the application’s concept, user experience, design and development. are.na

No Plans is a digital agency specialising in web design, brand identity and programming. Our background in design and our interactive expertise gives us a wider perspective, suited for special web projects, extensive branding challenges and digital strategy.

Based in London and New York, No Plans was founded in 2015 by Daniel Pianetti and Daniel Baer. Clients include:

  • Mario Testino+
  • Nowness
  • Pigalle
  • Zanzan
  • Grilli Type
  • Asger Carlsen
